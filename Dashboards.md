### 🔹 What It Should Show:

* Revenue (MTD / YTD)
* Expenses
* Gross Profit %
* Net Profit
* Budget vs Actual
* Top 5 cost drivers
* Monthly trend charts
* KPI cards at top

Create a dummy company like:

> “ABC Retail Pvt Ltd – Monthly Performance Report”

Make it look corporate.

This dashboard:

* Attracts founders
* Attracts finance managers
* Justifies ₹30k+ projects
